SVN switch:

To update working copy to a different URL
usage: switch URL [PATH]
*updates the working copy to point to a new URL.This is the svn way to move working copy
to a new branch.


Eg: 1. svn switch svn://ocodoco.mistralsoftware.com:2007 LAB
    

To switch back
Eg: 1. svn switch svn://ocodoco.mistralsoftware.com:2007/ ./
