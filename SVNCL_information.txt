SVN information:

To dispaly information about a local or remote item
usage: info [TARGET...]
*TARGET may be either a working-copy or URL


a.To display information with option -r (revision) with NUMBER
Eg: 1. svn info -r 70 
    2. svn info -r 87 test1.txt
    3. svn info -r 123 LAB


b.To display information with option -R (recursive) 
Eg: 1. svn info -R 
    2. svn info -R test1.txt
    3. svn info -R LAB/test2.txt
